About

I am a PhD Candidate in Computer Science whose research focuses on secure and trustworthy artificial intelligence, autonomous agents, and multi-agent systems. My background spans intelligence analysis, cyber operations, operations research, data science, and electronic warfare analysis. I study how AI-enabled systems behave in complex and adversarial environments, with the goal of translating rigorous technical research into secure, reliable, and operationally relevant capabilities.

Research

My current research examines the security and reliability of AI agents performing autonomous software-maintenance tasks.
